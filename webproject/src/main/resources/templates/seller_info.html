Frequently Asked Questions



What is GroupBuy? GroupBuys are consolidated bulk purchases of products, involving consumers in the same district coming together to place orders. Upon the product receiving a minimum number of orders, consumers will be able to purchase the item at a discounted price.
What are the benefits of GroupBuy? The GroupBuy model reduces cost and improves efficiency due to economies of scale, providing sellers with competitive advantage. GroupBuys typically receive a large number of orders, allowing sellers to take advantage of bulk purchases to lower the price of products.
In addition, by providing the option for pickup or delivery to a centralized location, GroupBuy enables sellers to significantly reduce delivery cost and time.
As a seller, what delivery services do I need to provide? There are 3 options available for your mode of delivery:
1. Delivery to individual buyers.
2. Deliver to community organizer or central location.
3. Pick up by the individual buyer or community organizer.
