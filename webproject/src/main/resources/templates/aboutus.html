Our Background

Founded in 2021, BevyBuy is Singapore’s most trusted group buy company.
BevyBuy is the go to source for great quality products, delivered right to your neighbourhood, at a discounted price. We work with both local and global suppliers, to bring the best deals right to your neighbourhood.

Our Mission

With our increasingly fast-paced lives today, convenience is increasingly important to consumers, to be able to better maximize time.
Hence, we are committed to connecting people with quality goods and services, at a discounted price, and ensuring seamless delivery of products right to your neighbourhood.

Our Values

At BevyBuy, we ensure equality and inclusion for everyone. Respect for one another is an everyday business practice.
In addition, with over 2000 suppliers on BevyBuy, we take great care in the work we do, accelerating growth of our people and businesses.
